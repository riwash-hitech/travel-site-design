Our Story

Crafting Himalayan Dreams Since 2010

RiwashTravel began with a simple mission: to share the unparalleled beauty of the Himalayas with travelers from around the world. What started as a small trekking company in Kathmandu has grown into a trusted name for Himalayan adventures.

Founded by a group of passionate mountaineers and local guides, we understand the mountains not just as a business, but as our home. Our deep-rooted connection to the region allows us to offer authentic experiences that go beyond typical tourist routes.

Over the past 15 years, we've had the privilege of guiding thousands of adventurers through Nepal, Tibet, Bhutan, and India. Each journey has enriched our understanding of what travelers seek – and we've continuously evolved to exceed those expectations.
