FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to visit Nepal is during spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) when the weather is clear and dry, perfect for trekking and sightseeing.
Most visitors can obtain a visa on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport in Kathmandu or at land border crossings. You can also apply for an e-visa before your trip.
Fitness requirements vary by trek. For moderate treks like Ghorepani Poon Hill, basic fitness is sufficient. For challenging treks like Everest Base Camp, good cardiovascular fitness and prior hiking experience are recommended.
Our packages typically include accommodation, meals during treks, experienced guides, permits, and transportation. International flights, personal gear, and travel insurance are usually not included.
