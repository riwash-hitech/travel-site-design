Our Story

Pioneering Himalayan Adventures Since 2009

Born from a shared passion for the mountains, Riwash Travel emerged when a group of local guides decided to share the magic of the Himalayas with the world. Our journey began with a simple belief: that trekking should be transformative, sustainable, and deeply connected to local communities.

Today, we've guided over 15,000 adventurers through Nepal's most spectacular landscapes, from the iconic trails of Everest to the hidden valleys of Upper Mustang. Every trek is crafted with the same care and expertise that defined our first expedition.
