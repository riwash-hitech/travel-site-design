FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trek with Riwash Travel?

You can book online through our website, send us an inquiry via the contact form, or call us directly. We'll guide you through the entire process.

What is included in the package price?

Our packages include accommodations, meals during the trek, permits, guide and porter services, and airport transfers. International flights and insurance are not included.

What is the best time to trek in Nepal?

The best seasons are Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November) when the weather is clear and stable with excellent mountain views.

Do I need travel insurance?

Yes, comprehensive travel insurance covering high-altitude trekking and emergency evacuation is mandatory for all our treks.
