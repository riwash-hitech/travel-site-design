Tour Overview

The Everest Base Camp trek is the ultimate bucket-list adventure for trekkers and nature enthusiasts. This 14-day journey takes you through the heart of the Khumbu region, offering breathtaking views of the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest (8,848m), Lhotse, Nuptse, and Ama Dablam.

You'll walk in the footsteps of legendary mountaineers, experience authentic Sherpa culture, visit ancient monasteries, and stand at the base of the world's highest mountain. The trek combines natural beauty with cultural immersion, making it one of the most rewarding adventures on Earth.

Highlights

  • Stand at Everest Base Camp (5,364m)
  • Hike to Kala Patthar (5,545m) for sunrise
  • Visit Tengboche Monastery
  • Explore Namche Bazaar

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)

Welcome to Nepal! Our representative will pick you up from the airport and transfer you to your hotel. Evening trek briefing and welcome dinner.

Day 02

Fly to Lukla, Trek to Phakding (2,610m)

Early morning scenic flight to Lukla (2,840m). Begin trekking through beautiful pine and rhododendron forests to Phakding. (3-4 hours trek)

Day 03

Phakding to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

Cross several suspension bridges including the famous Hillary Bridge. Steep ascent to Namche, the gateway to Everest. (6-7 hours trek)

Day 04

Acclimatization Day in Namche

Hike to Everest View Hotel for stunning mountain views. Visit the Sherpa Museum and explore Namche market. Essential acclimatization.

Day 05

Namche to Tengboche (3,867m)

Trek with spectacular views of Everest, Nuptse, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam. Visit the famous Tengboche Monastery. (5-6 hours trek)

Day 06

Tengboche to Dingboche (4,410m)

Descend through rhododendron forests, cross the Imja Khola, and gradual ascent to Dingboche. (5-6 hours trek)

Day 07

Acclimatization in Dingboche

Hike to Nagarjun Hill (5,100m) for panoramic views of Makalu, Lhotse, and Island Peak. Rest and acclimatize.

Day 08

Dingboche to Lobuche (4,940m)

Gradual climb past Thukla Pass with memorials to fallen climbers. Stunning views of Pumori and Nuptse. (5-6 hours trek)

Day 09

Lobuche to EBC (5,364m), Back to Gorak Shep

The big day! Trek to Everest Base Camp via the Khumbu Glacier. Celebrate your achievement! Return to Gorak Shep. (7-8 hours trek)

Day 10

Kala Patthar (5,545m), Descend to Pheriche

Early morning hike to Kala Patthar for breathtaking sunrise views of Everest. Descend to Pheriche. (7-8 hours trek)

Day 11

Pheriche to Namche Bazaar

Descend through Tengboche with easier walking. Return to the comforts of Namche. (6-7 hours trek)

Day 12

Namche to Lukla

Final trekking day. Descend to Lukla for celebration with your trekking crew. (6-7 hours trek)

Day 13

Fly to Kathmandu

Morning flight back to Kathmandu. Free day for rest, shopping, or sightseeing. Farewell dinner.

Day 14

Departure

Transfer to the airport for your departure. Namaste and see you again!
