Our Story

15 Years of Excellence

Founded in 2010, Riwash Travel began with a simple mission: to share the magic of the Himalayas with travelers from around the world. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Nepal's most trusted travel companies.

Our founders, born and raised in the shadow of the world's highest peaks, understood that the mountains were more than just a destination—they were a way of life. This deep connection to the land and its people has been the foundation of everything we do.

Today, we've helped over 50,000 travelers from 80+ countries experience the transformative power of the Himalayas. From the iconic Everest Base Camp to the mystical kingdom of Bhutan, we've guided adventurers through some of Earth's most spectacular landscapes.
