Trip Overview

Nepal is one of the most beautiful destinations in the world, with mountain scenery, lush green valleys, and spectacular landscapes welcoming you throughout the seasons. There are countless destinations for a panoramic experience, but this 6-day trip takes you to only the best.

Your Nepal trip starts with a short Kathmandu Valley sightseeing tour, which includes the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, and Patan. The trip lasts two days, and you'll get to visit numerous temples while also getting a view of each surrounding area you visit.

After exploring Kathmandu, you'll be escorted to the Nagarkot Hills, which offer a panoramic view of the Himalayas. Then, we'll take you to Bandipur, another hill station located in the Tanahun district. Once your short exploration of the valley is complete, the trip will take you to the lakeside city of Pokhara, where you will enjoy boat rides on Phewa Lake with stunning views of the Annapurna range.

Explore ancient temples, enjoy boat rides on Phewa Lake, visit the sacred Manakamana Temple via cable car, and create unforgettable memories with your loved one in the land of the Himalayas. This package is specially designed for couples seeking romance, adventure, and cultural experiences.

Detailed Itinerary

Upon arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, our representative will welcome you with traditional garlands and transfer you to your luxury hotel in Kathmandu. After check-in, enjoy a welcome drink and relax. In the evening, enjoy a romantic candlelight dinner at the hotel's rooftop restaurant with views of the city.

Airport Pickup Dinner 5-Star Hotel

After breakfast, start your day with a visit to Pashupatinath Temple, one of the most sacred Hindu temples. Continue to Boudhanath Stupa, the largest stupa in Nepal and a center of Tibetan Buddhism. Visit Kathmandu Durbar Square to see ancient palaces and temples. End the day at Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple) for panoramic city views at sunset.

UNESCO Sites Sightseeing

Early morning drive to Pokhara, Nepal's adventure capital. En route, take a cable car ride to Manakamana Temple, dedicated to the goddess who fulfills wishes. The cable car offers stunning views of the Trishuli River valley. Continue to Pokhara and check into your lakeside hotel. Evening free for leisure walk along Phewa Lake.

Scenic Drive Cable Car Lakeside

Early morning drive to Sarangkot for breathtaking sunrise views over the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges. Return to hotel for breakfast. Later, visit Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave, and the International Mountain Museum. Enjoy a romantic boat ride on Phewa Lake and visit Tal Barahi Temple located on an island. Evening free for shopping or relaxing by the lake.

Sunrise Boat Ride Mountain Views

After breakfast, drive to Nagarkot, a hill station famous for its sunrise and sunset views over the Himalayas. En route, visit Bhaktapur Durbar Square, known for its medieval architecture and pottery. Continue to Nagarkot and check into your resort. Evening enjoy the spectacular sunset over the Himalayan range including Mount Everest on clear days.

Bhaktapur Sunset View Himalayan Range

Early morning, enjoy the breathtaking sunrise over the Himalayas from your resort. After breakfast, drive back to Kathmandu. Depending on your flight time, you may have free time for last-minute shopping at Thamel. Our representative will transfer you to the airport for your onward journey with beautiful memories of your Nepal honeymoon.

Sunrise Airport Drop
