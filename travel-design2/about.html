About Riwash Travel

Your trusted partner for authentic Himalayan adventures since 2009. We create unforgettable trekking experiences with expert local guides, sustainable practices, and a deep passion for the mountains.

Our Story

Passionate About the Himalayas Since 2009

Founded by a group of experienced local guides, Riwash Travel began with a simple mission: to share the magic of the Himalayas with travelers from around the world while supporting the local communities we call home. We believe in responsible tourism that benefits both visitors and locals.

What started as a small team of passionate trekkers has grown into one of Nepal's most trusted trekking companies. Over the past 15 years, we've guided thousands of adventurers to Everest Base Camp, through the Annapurna Circuit, and to countless other breathtaking destinations across Nepal.

Our name "Riwash" comes from the local dialect meaning "path to the mountains" - and that's exactly what we provide: a well-prepared, safe, and memorable path to the world's greatest mountain experiences.
